About Me

My name is Josh Dean and I'm a User Experience Designer and Engineer residing in Salt Lake City, UT. I have experience in web design, user interface design, and HTML/CSS development. I really love what I do, which is creating websites and web applications that are polished in design and flawless in code (check out my portfolio).

Let me be a bit more specific: I optimize conversion rates, design user-friendly layouts, present important information clearly and remove unnecessary details.

I find a sense of satisfaction in building websites from scratch. I also feel that starting each project with a clean canvas is very important—that's why I never use templates or outdated code.
